Endsinger EX training game

Watch the sequence play out and wait for the 5 endsinger heads to appear. Click on the endsinger head you should stand under then wait.

  • The Arrow is the direction the middle endsinger is looking.
  • You have 9 seconds to select an endsinger or you lose.
  • You can change your answer by clicking on another endsinger.
  • The game will restart 5 seconds after being told correct or incorrect.
